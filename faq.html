Frequently Asked Questions

Are you accredited or licensed by educational organizations?
There isn’t always a need for licensing or accreditation for online courses. However, some programs do require it. Either way, people will usually ask what type of training or background you have, as well as any licensing that’s available if that’s relevant. Make sure that you answer this question by telling them if you do hold any licenses or accreditation, as well as by explaining whether or not those are needed, and why. It gives them the confidence that you have the right experience and boosts your credibility because you’re offering additional information for the sake of education alone.
Does online learning really work?
There are always going to be sceptics, and there are frequently multiple of them when it comes to online learning. Everyone wants to know if taking classes online "really works," but there are a lot of elements to take into account, making it difficult to answer. In general, internet education can be successful. That is contingent upon a number of factors, including the appropriate arrangement of the courses, the expertise and education of the provider, and the student's performance in an online learning environment. People need to know what they're getting into, and you should be able to convey these things to them. After that, you might ask them to get in touch with you to talk about whether it would be suitable for them.
What is online learning like?
The most common query from those who have never used online learning is what it's really like. You are aware that there are various forms of online learning, thus there is a warning. Thus, this is your opportunity to clarify it to others. Make it clear to them that there are several kinds of online programmes and courses, and they must choose the one that best suits their needs. After that, give them all the information they require by outlining the kinds of courses you provide and the operation of your online programmes. In addition to providing them with facts, this builds their trust because you're being open and honest with them.
Can I learn at my own pace?
This is yet another typical query regarding online education. Self-paced programmes are frequently offered in addition to conventional classroom or meeting-style programmes. Because there are so many busy people in the world today who desire to improve themselves, self-paced programmes are very popular. Regardless of whether you offer this choice, make sure you respond to this query and outline the advantages and disadvantages of each method of instruction. As an added benefit, you can ask them to get in touch with you to find out more details or to talk through their alternatives.
What types of assignments do you give?
People frequently want to know what kind of work they'll be doing when they take online classes, much like they want to know the style of online learning. Make sure to include a brief overview of your qualifications here, and if you can, include links to earlier tasks or projects. Links to programme or course pages are also possible. People can make better decisions about their educational needs if you tell them about the coursework expectations and the way the courses are taught.
What are the requirements to take the courses or sign up with your website?
People will be curious about how to begin. Although you can direct them to the sections of your website that assist them in that process, you should also take the time to explain it to them directly. List any enrollment windows you may have, for example. If there are any requirements for deposits or payment, please clarify. Regardless of the nature of the courses you offer online, make sure customers are aware of all the requirements.
What are the technology requirements?
In a same spirit, consumers must ascertain the state of their computers. You should describe all of the software you utilise as well as the sort of equipment required for participants in your online courses. Include any programmes or apps that are utilised in this section. Here, too, you should provide technology if you have it or point them in the direction of the right buy. Assure them that you are available to assist with the needs and that they may get in touch with you to talk about whether their system can support the requirements of your online courses.
How long does each course take?
While some online courses span several months, others are only a few hours long. Your courses could have different durations based on the kind of online education you provide. Tell folks about the many kinds of courses you provide and the duration of each one. This is another opportunity to remind customers that you provide self-paced learning and reassure them that you can assist them in studying at their own speed.
